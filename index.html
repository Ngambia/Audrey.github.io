01

Research Profile

Early-career researcher bridging the gap between atomistic molecular modelling and industrial Greenhouse Gas Removal (GGR). My work is defined by building open-access computational infrastructure designed for community adoption — molecular models of biochar with realistic, controlled porosity that let researchers move from trial-and-error to predictive design. Currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the UK Biochar Research Centre (UKBRC), I lead the translation of mechanistic modelling insights into operational protocols for carbon removal verification, in collaboration with Cargill. My trajectory combines high-performance computing with policy-aligned industrial deployment.
